Design and development of a portfolio for an artist.
2018

The Project

A platform to help an artist promote their make-up artistry work, get exposure and more clientele.

Barbora.co

My Role

My role was to design and develop a website accordingly. I’ve also shot and prepared the assets used on the site.

The Problem

Build a platform for an artist to host their work and gain more exposure which will lead into more clientele.

The Solution

Barbora.co main page Concept V1

By designing and developing a platform for the client that’s available online will ensure communication with new clients and increase the chances of a collaboration with photographers and models. This will then lead to increase in exposure.

Having a website that contains the most recent / best work will help visitors understand the clients work and see what they excel at.

In addition, providing a section where visitors of the site can learn about the artist will aid with their understanding about the client experience, knowledge and passion when it comes to working with them.

TLDR - Design and develop a portfolio that showcases their work, describe their work ethics and passion in ‘about me’ section and to increase conversion rates by providing a contact form

Landing Page

A good way to present something is by giving it a whole page to describe itself. Landing pages - especially in portfolios are very important as they describe the client, describe their work and gives the visitor to explore more.

Providing a portrait of the client and their title with CTA buttons will ensure clarity.
