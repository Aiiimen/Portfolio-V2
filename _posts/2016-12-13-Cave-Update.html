Having a dual setup requires alot of space. Specially when you have 2 24inch monitors, a huge razer keyboard and require some space for your mouse. Not to mention the computer itself. I can also place it on the floor but that's not really ideal because it will collect dust really quick.

being an old fashioned person, I still love my pen and paper and just a huge space where I can sketch and write my ideas down. There's this famous saying "First, Solve the problem, then write the code". That can be applied to nearly everything I do. Even photography, when it comes to self portraits. I decided to fix together couple of desks from IKEA and take over one side of my room. So, in the end I got myself a desk that's 2.4m long.

Felt pretty bad for not posting because I've been super busy changing things around in my room but that's still not an excuse. I represent to you a small part of my 2.40m long desk that I call "Light Bulb". It is my thinking area. Blog post coming soon :)
