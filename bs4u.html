Best Skills 4 U

Design and development of a website for a construction company.
March 2019

The Project

To design and develop a website for BESTSKILLS4U showcasing their success, promote their services and introducing them to construction industry.

bestskills4u.com

The Project

- Conducted research regarding the construction industry
- Design the User experience for the site
- Design the user Interface
- Develop the website

Overview

For the past 8 years BestSkills4u has been helping student gain their qualification in the construction industry to help them progress in their career. The have enrolled over 400 students with 89% recommendation and 95% pass rate.
BestSkills4U also provide construction and engineering services where they assist with construction of sites. By having experienced staff and true specialists’ employers – they also provide consulting.
Bestskilsl4u approached me as they wanted to expand their business and promote their courses and training they provide.

The Solution

To tick all the requirements and make sure I represent BestSKills4U effectively – I spread the design over 5 pages.

Known as a scrolling telling – this is one page where it holds all the information needed to tell a story about a product or in this case, a company.
